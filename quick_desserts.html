Bob Andy - Honey


  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 8 hours 10 mins
  • Shelf Life: 2 weeks in freezer
  • Equipment: Blender

    Quick & Dirty Recipe Directions

  1. Throw all ingredients into your high-speed blender and blend, starting very slowly and then increasing the speed. Keep blending until smooth, like a creamy chocolate fudge with no cashew chunks.
  2. Pour mixture into the heart-shaped pans/molds and place in freezer.
  3. Freeze until firm (8 hours).
  4. Store in freezer at all times.

Ingredients (yields 12 servings)

  • 2 ½ C cashews
  • ½ C cocoa powder
  • ½ C maple syrup
  • ¼ C coconut oil
  • 1 t pure vanilla extract
  • 2 T lemon juice
  • 1/8 t sea salt
  • ¾ C water
