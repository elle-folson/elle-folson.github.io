Chaka Khan - My Funny Valentine


Enjoy this healthy raw, vegan, gluten free, grain free, refined sugar free brownie with chocolate and raspberry cream!

    Take It Slow Recipe Directions

  1. Grind the almonds down to flour with a food processor. Add the dates, cacao powder, and vanilla. Process until the dates are completely broken down. Add the melted cacao paste and process to incorporate. Press the dough into a plastic wrap lined bread pan (3 x 5 inches).
  2. Blend all but the coconut oil in a high speed blender until smooth. Add the oil and blend to incorporate. Pour the cream over the brownies and spread evenly. Chill in the freezer for 6-8 hours and then transfer to the fridge for at least 6 hours.
  3. Sift the cacao powder into a bowl. Add all the remaining ingredients and whisk to combine. The mixture should be smooth. If it starts to get too thick, set it over a bowl of hot water while whisking. Drizzle over the brownies. Chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour. Slice into 8 pieces.
  4. Grind the goji berries in a coffee grinder. Sprinkle both berries over the brownies.

Ingredients (yields 8 servings)

  • 2/3 C almonds
  • 2/3 C pitted, packed dates - chopped
  • 1 1/2 T cacao powder
  • 1/4 t vanilla bean powder
  • 1 T + 1 t melted cacao paste
  • 1 C raspberries
  • 1/3 C cashews
  • 2 T coconut flakes
  • 3 T maple syrup
  • 1/2 t lemon juice
  • 4 T melted coconut oil
  • 2 T maple syrup
  • 1 T raw cashew or almond butter
  • 1 T + 1 t cacao powder
  • 1 T melted coconut oil
  • 1 T goji berries
  • Fresh or frozen raspberries
